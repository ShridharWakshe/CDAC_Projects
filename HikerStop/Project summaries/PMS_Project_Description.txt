Title- SquareFeets
The main purpose is to develop a Real Estate Property Portal to make 
online platform where Buyers
 & Builders can exchange information about properties quickly,  
effectively and inexpensively at 
one place.
It helps in building a website to 
1. Advertise a property for Builders. 
2. Search for a property, browse through properties, schedule a site 
visit as per available slots from Builders

Technologies used- MySql, Springboot, ReactJS, J2EE
